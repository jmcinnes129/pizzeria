About Us

In the bustling streets of Naples, Italy, in the year 1642, a passionate and visionary pizzaiolo named Giuseppe Bianchi had a dream that would forever change the course of pizza history. Giuseppe, known for his unconventional ideas and commitment to tradition, founded a pizzeria like no other - Pineapple-less Pizzeria. The idea for Pineapple-less Pizzeria originated from Giuseppe's desire to preserve the authenticity of the Neapolitan pizza, which he believed was being threatened by foreign influences. At the time, exotic ingredients like pineapples were unheard of in traditional Italian cuisine, and Giuseppe was determined to keep it that way.

As the years went by, Pineapple-less Pizzeria weathered challenges and changes in the culinary landscape. It became a symbol of resistance against culinary fads, and its commitment to tradition turned it into a beloved landmark in the heart of Naples.

In the present day, Pineapple-less Pizzeria stands as a living testament to Giuseppe Bianchi's vision. The pizzeria remains true to its roots, serving authentic Neapolitan pizza to locals and tourists alike. It has become a must-visit destination for those who appreciate the history, tradition, and craftsmanship behind a truly classic Italian pizza experience. The legacy of Pineapple-less Pizzeria continues, reminding the world that sometimes, the best pizzas are the ones that stick to the basics.
